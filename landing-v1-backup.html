THE GAME

3 arrows. One life.

TowerFall-style archer combat: no respawns, and every heart on the map is up for grabs — including yours. Your rivals are AI that dodge, catch arrows and hunt you down. Each fighter has its own ultimate on the R key.
1

SHOOT

You start with 3 arrows. They stick where they land: go pick them up, or DODGE into an arrow to catch it mid-air. Aim in 8 directions.

2

STEAL

Hit someone and they drop a heart on the floor. Scoop it up. The richer you get, the bigger the target on your back.

3

SURVIVE

The storm shrinks the safe zone round after round until it's a 1v1. Whoever's left banks everything they're carrying.
